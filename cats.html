My history with cats.

I always wanted cats since I was a little girl and we used to live in apartments so we could never get one. Until we moved into our first house then we were able to get two kittens name Storm and Dash. Stormie ran away when he accidently got out after two years. Dashie died at the age of 12 of cancer in 2016. At this point, we had my Aunt's cat Ptah (named after an Eyptian god) and Solo. Then, Ptah died at the age of 17 of cancer in 2018. When my babies leave me, it really hurts hard but I want to adopt and love as many cats as possible so I don't let the pain win over my love of cats.
